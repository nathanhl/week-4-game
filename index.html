Pokemon Collectors

Here's how to play the game!


  1. The game will assign you a number to achieve
  2. Below you will find four wild Pokemon!

    • Charmander
    • Jolteon
    • Bulbasaur
    • Mudkip

  3. Each crystal has a specific point value that will be added to your total score after each time they are clicked.

  4. You win by matching your total score to the random number that the game picks for you.

  5. You lose if your total score exceeds the random number that was generated.

  6. You will not know the value of each crystal until you click on it!

  7. The value of each crystal will change each time you play the game.

  8. Good luck and have fun!
